Flooring

What to Expect

New floors change everything. The way a room sounds, the way it feels underfoot, the way guests notice it the moment they walk in. Whether you're replacing worn carpet, upgrading to hardwood, or laying luxury vinyl throughout — Hutch & Co. installs it clean, level, and built to last. When we're done, the floors make the room.

Specific Services

  • Hardwood flooring installation — nail-down and glue-down
  • Engineered hardwood installation
  • Luxury vinyl plank (LVP) and luxury vinyl tile (LVT) installation
  • Laminate flooring installation
  • Floating floor installation over existing subfloor
  • Subfloor repair and leveling before install
  • Transition strip and threshold installation
  • Baseboard and quarter-round shoe molding installation after flooring
  • Carpet removal and disposal
  • Old flooring tear-out and disposal
  • Tile flooring installation in kitchens and bathrooms
  • Grouting, sealing, and finishing

Pricing

Free, upfront quotes before any work begins. No surprises, no hidden fees. You'll always know what you're paying.

Blinds & Window Treatments

The right window treatments pull a room together — and the wrong install ruins them. Crooked blinds, brackets that won't hold, cords that jam on day one. Hutch & Co. measures, mounts, and installs your blinds and shades properly the first time, so every window looks intentional and every treatment operates exactly as it should.

Specific Services

  • Horizontal blind installation — wood, faux wood, and aluminum
  • Vertical blind installation
  • Roller shade and solar shade installation
  • Roman shade installation
  • Cellular and honeycomb shade installation
  • Motorized blind and shade installation
  • Curtain rod and drapery hardware installation
  • Curtain and drape hanging
  • Inside-mount and outside-mount installation
  • Blind and shade replacement on existing hardware
  • Repair of broken tilt mechanisms, cords, and brackets
  • Multi-window same-day installation

Trim Work

What to Expect

Trim is what separates a finished home from a truly polished one. Clean mitered corners, baseboards that sit flush to the floor, door casings that frame a room like a picture frame. Most people don't notice trim when it's done right — they just feel it. Hutch & Co. installs and finishes trim work to a standard that makes every room look built with intention.

Specific Services

  • Baseboard installation — standard, colonial, and craftsman profiles
  • Door casing and window casing installation
  • Crown molding installation
  • Chair rail installation
  • Wainscoting and board-and-batten installation
  • Picture rail and wall panel molding
  • Window stool and apron installation
  • Shoe molding and quarter-round installation
  • Coping and miter cuts for inside and outside corners
  • Caulking and nail hole filling before paint
  • Trim repair — replacement of damaged or rotted sections
  • Trim installation after new flooring

Pricing

Free, upfront quotes before any work begins. No surprises, no hidden fees. You'll always know what you're paying.

Painting & Finishing

What to Expect

Fresh paint is the fastest transformation in a home. Sharp lines at the trim, smooth walls without roller texture or lap marks, ceilings that look bright instead of dingy. Whether you're refreshing before a sale or finally making a space feel like yours, the difference a clean paint job makes is immediate and undeniable — and Hutch & Co. does it right.

Specific Services

  • Full interior room painting — walls and ceilings
  • Accent wall painting
  • Baseboard, door casing, and window trim painting
  • Interior door painting — both sides and edges
  • Ceiling-only painting
  • Closet and small space painting
  • Touch-up painting after repairs or patching
  • Cabinet exterior painting (not refinishing)
  • Caulking along trim, windows, and baseboards before paint
  • Light drywall repair and prep before painting
  • Paint color consultation and sample testing
  • Pre-sale paint refresh and whole-home touch-up

Pricing

Free, upfront quotes before any work begins. No surprises, no hidden fees. You'll always know what you're paying.

Get a Free Quote

Heads Up

We handle interior painting. Exterior painting, full cabinet refinishing, and wallpaper removal are outside our scope — just ask and we'll be straight with you.

Custom Projects

Not every project fits a category. Sometimes it's a built-in you've been sketching on paper for two years. Sometimes it's a mudroom that needs to actually function, a closet that needs to be rethought, or a space that just needs someone willing to figure it out with you. Hutch & Co. is that someone. Bring the idea — we'll build a plan and get it done.

Examples of Custom Work

  • Built-in shelving and storage systems
  • Mudroom bench, hooks, and cubby installation
  • Closet organization and shelving build-out
  • Laundry room shelving and folding station
  • Home office built-in desk and shelving
  • Pantry shelving and pull-out systems
  • Garage shelving and wall organization
  • Accent walls — shiplap, board-and-batten, or panel systems
